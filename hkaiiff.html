Entry Requirements

HKAIIFF is the first festival to enforce copyright via blockchain code.

01

Identity Verification (SBT)

All "Digital Actors" featured in the film must have a valid StarOS SBT (Soulbound Token). If you use an unauthorized likeness (Deepfake), the TEE audit node will automatically reject the submission.

02

Asset Transparency

Directors must submit the "Asset Hash List" used in generation (e.g., LoRA models, Music stems). All assets must be licensed via AIF or be Public Domain.

03

AIF Entry Fee

A submission fee of 100 AIF is required. This fee goes into the DAO pool to fund the "Audience Choice Award".
